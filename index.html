ABOUT ME

I am Yusuf Yavuz. I was born in 2002 in Istanbul, Turkey. I grew up and currently reside on the Anatolian side of Istanbul.

I completed my high school education at Ümraniye Anatolian Imam-Hatip High School. For my university education, I graduated from the Computer Programming department at Atatürk University. I am actively learning and improving my English skills.

I initially started my journey in software development in October 2021, focusing on front-end development. Through online courses, I learned the fundamentals of JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. Later on, I expanded my skills by working with Vue.js and the Quasar framework to develop single-page applications.

From May 2022 onwards, I delved into the C# realm through online training, honing my skills in Unity for prototyping and game development.

I am currently actively developing myself in the Unity engine, focusing on indie games, hyper-casual games, and prototyping. I strive to improve my skills in object-oriented programming (OOP) and design patterns to write clean code.

I continue to enhance my skills in both Blueprint and C++ in the Unreal Engine.
